A Glimpse of Sri Lanka

Sigiriya

Rising from the jungle like a giant stone flame, Sigiriya: the legendary Lion Rock guards the secrets of a vanished kingdom. Its painted maidens, hidden gardens, and sky high ruins whisper tales of power, beauty, and betrayal. Few places on earth blend mystery and majesty quite like this ancient fortress in the clouds.
